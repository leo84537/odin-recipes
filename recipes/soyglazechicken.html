Soy Glazed Chicken Recipe

Home

Recipe Steps:

This is a recipe for easy peasy soy glazed chicken


Ingredients:

  • CHICKEN THIGHS
  • SALT N PEPPA
  • CORNSTARCH
  • VEGGIE OIL
  • SOY SAUCE
  • OYSTER SAUCE
  • HONEY
  • GARLIC CLOVES
  • FIVE SPICE POWDER

Steps to GREATNESS:

  1. Season chicken - salt and pepper
  2. Coat chicken in cornstarch
  3. Fry of both sides for 4 mins each
  4. Make sauce - soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, garlic, five spice powder -
  5. Let sauce simmer 30 secs in that chicky oil
  6. Add chicken and cook on high heat for 1-2 mins
  7. EAT N ENJOY
